Global Warming - InfoVis

An InfoVis project about climate change by Gaetano Bonofiglio and Veronica Iovinella.

The purpose of this small website is to show causes and effects of climate change over the years. The focus will be on CO2 emissions by country and year, and also their correlation with global warming and the effect that it is producing on land ice mass and water levels.

The charts were made with D3.js and Three.js libraries. The data is taken mainly from NASA, NOAA CSIRO and CDIAC.
